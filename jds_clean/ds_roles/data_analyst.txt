Create dashboards and reports using Excel, Tableau, Power BI, or similar tools
Perform statistical analysis to support business decision-making
Develop and maintain automated reporting systems
Collaborate with stakeholders to understand data requirements and KPIs
Identify data quality issues and work with IT teams to resolve them
Present findings and recommendations to management and cross-functional teams

Bachelor's degree in Statistics, Mathematics, Economics, or related field
2+ years of experience in data analysis or business intelligence
Advanced proficiency in Excel including pivot tables, VLOOKUP, and macros
Strong SQL skills for data querying and analysis
Experience with data visualization tools like Tableau, Power BI, or QlikView
Knowledge of statistical analysis and basic machine learning concepts
Strong analytical thinking and problem-solving skills
Excellent written and verbal communication skills

Preferred:

Experience with Python or R for data analysis
Knowledge of database management systems
Familiarity with business intelligence platforms
Experience in specific industry domain
